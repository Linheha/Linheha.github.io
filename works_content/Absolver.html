ABSOLVER

Role: UI/UX designer

Absolver is a combat RPG based on martial art.
The player can create his own combos through a combat deck editor, by combining differents attacks and assigning them to 4 stances.


From April 2016 to March 2018, I worked as sole UI/UX designer in close collaboration with the art director and the lead game designer.

My job was to design the whole UI: menus, HUD, icons and integrate and animate them on Unreal Engine 4 using Unreal Motion Graphics tool.

-
Released on August 2017
Developed on UE4 for PC/PS4/Xbox by Sloclap
Published by Devolver Digital

HUD

The HUD is composed by the health gauge on top left of the screen, the powers and weapon the player has equipped on the bottom left, the Stamina and the "Perfect Link" combined in one gauge on the bottom center and the stance indicator just under it.
Level up notification
Reward notification

MENU

Most of the menus are available while pressing Start button, but all the menus are available when you sit at an Altar:
Meditation
Meditation menu focuses on your character constitution, it allows you to improve your character attributes, change your combat style, and perfect your combos.
Combo Editor
You can access the combo editor menu via the "Practice" button. Then, you will be able to edit your combo by equipping attacks for the four stances.
Equipment
Equipment menu allows you to customize the appearance of your character, and to equip a weapon and two powers.
