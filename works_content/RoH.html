ROAD OF HOPE

Role: 2D/3D artist

Road of Hope is a narrative road trip in which you embody Ada a bush-taxi driver. The game takes place entirely in the closed doors of a makeshift truck moving along in a landscape ravaged by natural disaster and war.


During 6 months, I collaborated with 2 other artists and made 3D assets (the vegetation, the truck, some buildings and objects), 2D assets (map, icons), the logo, some concept arts.

-
Enjmin student project
Developed on UE4
Team - Bastien Besland, Nicolas Leroy, Anne Bournisien, Ronaldo Choa Piane, Téninké Camara, Mathilda Kallouch Neuville, Maxime Lidolff, Tony Salamon, Basile Bastian, Logan Salmon
