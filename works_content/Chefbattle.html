CHEF BATTLE

Role: UI/UX designer

Chef Battle is a mobile game in which two players compete against each other to make the tastier and the better looking dish. They have to choose the right ingredients and the right techniques to satisfy the jury.


From July to December 2018, I worked as UI/UX designer in close collaboration with the UI artist, the art director and the game designers.

I mostly focused on the functional part of the interfaces, making them clear and making sure that the overall user experience progresses without friction.

-
Announced in October 2018
Developed on Unity for mobile (iOS & Android) by Nolaroads
